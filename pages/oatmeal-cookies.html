Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

A delicious recipe great for any occasion

Ingredients:

  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Oats

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F. In large bowl, stir brown sugar and butter until blended. Stir in vanilla and egg until light and fluffy. Stir in oats, flour, baking soda and salt; stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
  2. Onto ungreased cookie sheet, drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart.
  3. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Cool slightly; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.
